Past Continuous Tense

📚 What is Past Continuous?

The Past Continuous tense describes an action that was happening at a specific time in the past. It shows an ongoing or continuous action that was interrupted or happening while something else took place.

Example: "I was studying when the phone rang"

The studying was happening continuously until it was interrupted.

🔧 Structure

Subject + was/were + Verb + -ing

✅ Examples

  • •
    It was raining all morning. (Estaba lloviendo toda la mañana)
  • •
    The sun was shining brightly. (El sol brillaba intensamente)
  • •
    They were playing while the wind was blowing. (Estaban jugando mientras el viento soplaba)
  • •
    I was walking when lightning was flashing. (Estaba caminando cuando había rayos)

💡 When to Use

🔄 Simultaneous Actions

Two actions happening at the same time in the past

⚡ Interrupted Actions

"I was studying when the phone rang"

📝 Descriptions

"The weather was getting cold"

🎬 Background

"It was raining when we arrived"
