Past Continuous Tense

📚 What is Past Continuous?

The Past Continuous tense describes an action that was happening at a specific time in the past. It shows an ongoing or continuous action that was interrupted or happening while something else took place.

Example: "I was studying when the phone rang"

📖 Structure:

Subject + was/were + Verb + -ing

✅ Examples:

  • • It was raining all morning.
  • • The sun was shining brightly.
  • • They were playing while the wind was blowing.
  • • She was studying while it was snowing.
